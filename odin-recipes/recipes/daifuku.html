Strawberry Daifuku

Description

Soft Mochi filled with Red Bean Paste and Fresh Strawberries. Made from glutinous rice into mochi with filling.

Thank you to Catherine Zhang for this wonderful recipe!

Ingredients

  • 600g Red Bean Paste
  • 200g Glutinous Rice Flour
  • 65g Granulated Sugar
  • 40g Cornstarch
  • 360ml Full Cream Milk
  • Pink Food Colouring
  • 1.5tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 8 Large Strawberries

Steps

  1. Combine the glutinous rice flour, sugar, and cornstarch in a medium-sized microwave-safe bowl, and whisk to combine
  2. Add the milk and food coloring, and whisk until smooth
  3. Cover the mochi mixture with cling wrap and microwave for 2 minutes on high
  4. Uncover and stir
  5. Then re-cover and microwave for another 2 minutes, the mochi dough should be semi-translucent
  6. If the mochi is still slightly liquidy/milky re-cover it and microwave at 1-minute intervals until cooked through the mochi to cool enough to handle
  7. Add the vegetable oil and using gloved or oiled hands knead until smooth
  8. Prepare a baking tray lined with baking paper
  9. Divide the mochi into 8 equal portions and place on the baking sheet
  10. Flatten mochi and place a ball of red bean paste in the middle
  11. Bring the edges of the mochi together and roll it into a ball
  12. Dust in cornstarch
  13. Use a sharpe knife to make an cut at the top of the mochi
  14. Wash and trim strawberries
  15. Place strawberries into each cut
  16. Enjoy!
