Bibimbap

Bibimbap is a healthy Korean dish comprising of rice, finely sliced mixed vegetables, egg and sauce, served on a piping hot stone bowl. It is spicy and sweet and easy to prepare.

Thank you to the Minimalist Baker for the recipe.

Ingredients

  • 150g white rice
  • 45ml seasame oil
  • 360ml water
  • 1 pinch sea salt
  • 110g bean sprouts
  • 3 large handfuls of mixed vegetables, finely sliced
  • 2 stalks green onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 30ml tamari
  • 2 large eggs
  • 60g gochujang sauce

Steps

  1. Cook rice in ricecooker
  2. Heat a large saucepan over medium heat, coat pan in seasame oil
  3. Once oil is hot, add vegetables one type at a time in batches, and cook until lightly browned
  4. Remove vegetables and set aside in their batches
  5. Cook both eggs sunny side up, with the yolk still runny and set aside
  6. To serve, add a portion of rice and top with cooked vegetables and egg
  7. Add 1-2tbsp of gochujang sauce and mix well
  8. Enjoy!
